Directions

Our Address

Anchorage Quay, Salford M50

Via Tram

We highly recommend travelling by tram if possible. We are right in front of the Anchorage Tram Stop in Zone 2.

Parking

Four hours FREE parking at the Lowry Outlet Car Park when purchasing a Cineverse ticket. Please ensure that your ticket is validated at Cineverse. After the 4 hours you will be charged normal rate each subsequent hour will be charged £1 an hour up to 4 hours, a full list of charges can be found on our tariff boards.

Accessibility

There is some closer parking space on site if you have a Disabled Parking Badge. Please get in touch to ask about any specific requirements that might make your visit easier, we're happy to help!
